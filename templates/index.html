About

Thanks for your interest in bldg. We are currently in alpha, meaning don't be shocked if you come back later and eveything is entirely different. As we progress through the finalish stages of development, we can't get enough of your input, so please send any thoughts, suggestions, or cease and desist orders to mdietz@wesleyan.edu and I'll be sure to take it under advisement. This project's goal is to provide users with a high quality of information about not only the big-name buildings but about anything fascinating people see in their areas. We want to provide this information free of charge back to the users (and are almost shocked this information is not already out in the 'net) so our database will be made available as soon as we fully launch, so we can ensure that the quality of information is high; however, feel free to use any information from this alpha period however you would like, but we place no guarantee on its accuracy or usefulness in conversation. Please enjoy and feel free to contribute freely but keep in mind to only upload images that are your own or have been provided without a license, and if your image has been uploaded without your removal, please email me and it will be taken down.
